# Man_Shift_App

Man_Shift is a Flutter-based mobile application designed to help users challenge toxic masculinity and build healthier digital habits. The app delivers daily micro-lessons, actionable tips, reflection prompts, and an AI-powered chat assistant designed to guide personal growth and digital responsibility.

## Features

- **Daily Lessons**  
  Short, powerful micro-lessons teaching respect, digital consent, empathy, and accountability.

- **Actionable Tips**  
  Every lesson includes practical steps users can apply immediately in their online interactions.

- **Reflection Prompts**  
  Thought-provoking questions to encourage self-evaluation and growth.

- **AI Chat Assistant**  
  A builtâ€‘in AI chat feature that helps users ask questions, reflect, get guidance, or deepen lesson understanding. *Not a community or peer chat.*

- **Streak Tracking**  
  Build consistent habits with a visual display of weekly lesson completion.

- **Dark Mode**  
  Switch between light and dark themes for comfortable reading anytime.

- **Profile & Progress**  
  View your completed lessons, streak history, and reflections in one place.
